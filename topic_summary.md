Placeholder summary for the Dermapen manuscript workspace.

The project concerns ex vivo human skin delivery after pretreatment with an electrically powered microneedling pen. The uploaded manuscript frames Dermapen-generated microchannels as the primary mechanism enabling deeper intraskin loading of liposome-associated tracer signal, free 70 kDa dextran, and 1 micrometer fluorescent particulate model systems. It also treats brief topical massage after application as a practical secondary enhancer that may improve loading into freshly generated channels.

Working paper message, to be refined later: physical barrier bypass by standardized microneedling appears more important for deep delivery than conventional liposome composition, whereas formulation-dependent effects are more evident when the skin barrier remains intact.
